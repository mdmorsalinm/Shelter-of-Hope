Our Mission
At Shelter of Hope, our mission is to provide support, resources, and shelter to individuals experiencing
homelessness. We strive to create a safe environment where everyone has the opportunity to rebuild their
lives.
Our Story
Shelter of Hope was founded by two passionate software engineering majors from Brooklyn Tech, Jason and
Morsalin. Driven by our desire to make a tangible difference in our community, we combined our technical
skills and compassionate hearts to establish Shelter of Hope. We believe that everyone deserves a place
to call home, and we are dedicated to making that a reality.
